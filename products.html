Our Gym Memberships:

Basic Gym Membership - $30/Month

The Basic Gym Membership is the standard gym membership. This includes all day access to our gym everyday. People who buy the Basic Gym membership will also be able to have wifi included. All members will also have access to all of our equipment, our sauna, our pool, and our locker room.

Intermediate Gym Membership - $40/Month

This Gym Membership will include everything of the previous membership, however with this membership you will also be allowed to bring one guest with you each time you visit the gym. All members will also have access to all of our equipment, our sauna, our pool, and our locker room.

Advanced Gym Membership - $50/Month

The Advanced Gym Membership is a great option for many looking to change themslevs. Our Advanced membership includes everything listed in the previous memberships while also including much more. The Advanced Gym Membership comes packed with 24 hour access, child care services, and discounted protein drinks. All members will also have access to all of our equipment, our sauna, our pool, and our locker room.

Ultimate Gym Membership - $60/Month

The Ultimate Gym Membership is our highest offer membership. This membership comes with everything listed in the previous membership while also including one on one training every week with our personally appointed gym trainors. This would be a great option for many people. All members will also have access to all of our equipment, our sauna, our pool, and our locker room.
